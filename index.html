My name is Max Goh. I graduated from Ngee Ann Polytechnic on 2013 with a Diploma in Electronics & Computer Engineering. I have then spent the next 2 years serving the military as an Infantry Sergeant. I have been working as a Software Engineer for Trinax PTE LTD ever since.

Why Carousell?

I have always wanted to work in a SaaS start-up where I can focus on working on projects that provides a valuable service to the community. My previous company, Trinax PTE LTD (which was great) was a consulting business where I spent much time facing clients and building web-applications that were mostly event-focused and would be tore down in a couple of days or weeks time-span. I personally have used Carousell for some purchase on some tech gears, and I sincerely believe in it's market potential. I have a feeling that Carousell has a positive and socialiable community and I would be able to help Carousell grow just as much as I will.
